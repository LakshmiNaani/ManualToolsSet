✦ AI Assist — Options

Configure your AI provider. Settings are saved in Chrome and apply to all your local HTML tools.

One-time setup: Allow file:// access

Chrome requires you to manually enable file access for this extension:

  1. Go to chrome://extensions
  2. Find AI Assist — ManualToolsSet
  3. Click Details → enable "Allow access to file URLs"

Privacy Notice

Your API key is stored in Chrome's encrypted storage (chrome.storage.sync) and is never written to any HTML file or localStorage.

When you click an AI feature, your task/checklist/log data is sent to the configured AI provider. For complete privacy, use Ollama (local, no data leaves your machine).
